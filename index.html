Welcome to Concentration

Three powerful tools — hover or tap each for details:

  • ▦ Eisenhower Matrix + Pomodoro
    Plan tasks by urgency & importance in the 4-quadrant matrix. Tap a task to start a 25-min Work → 5-min Break cycle (15-min long break every 4 rounds).
  • ▥ Vertical Time Gauge
    Calendar-style timeline of your day. See exactly when you worked on what; click any colored band to edit its start/end times.
  • ● Focus Score
    Measure your focus level by external factors (internet, devices, location) and internal factors (sleep, emotional wellness, energy). Pixel-art houses & faces visualize your score.

Concentration — User Manual

Setup page

Plan your tasks — The Eisenhower Matrix divides tasks into four quadrants: Important · Urgent (yellow), Important · Not Urgent (green), Not Important · Urgent (blue), Not Important · Not Urgent (pink). Add tasks with the + add task button, type a name, press Enter to add a new row, Backspace on an empty row to delete it. Drag the ≡ handle to reorder within a quadrant. On phone, tap the ⤢ toggle above the matrix to enlarge cells; swipe sideways to reach the right-side cells.

Time Start & Goals — Starting Date & Time: when your focus session begins. The vertical time gauge starts 30 minutes before this. Tap Now to set to current time. I'll get things done by and I'm going to sleep at: target times shown as countdowns and as blue lines on the gauge. +30m and +1h buttons add to the existing target (cumulative). The × button clears each field.

Focus level — based on factors — Two sliders (External, Internal) auto-compute from selected chips, sliders, and Others entries. The default-max formula is 100 − (sum of max possible scores), so reaching 100 means everything good is selected. Sleep slider is 0–10 (caps at 7 if > 9). Sleep and Emotional Wellness contribute ×3 to the internal score. Slider numbers are blue when positive, red when negative, black at zero.

Motivational quotes — One quote per line. The top bar shows a random one re-picked once an hour.

Focus page

Vertical time gauge — White calendar-style column on the left with hour grid lines, hour labels, a thin red current-time line with a rounded badge, and blue lines for "Go home" (deadline) and "Sleep" (bedtime). Range: 30 min before Starting Time → 12 hours after. Color-coded bands appear when timers run; click any band to edit its start/end times in a popup.

Internal & External score cards — On the top right. Pixel-art faces (Internal) and houses (External) change with your score across 7 levels (0–27 → image 0, …, 73–100 → image 6). The bar marker reflects the score as a percentage from 0 (left) to 100 (right). Below: overall score + a message ("You're amazing." / "You're almost in the zone." / etc.).

Pomodoro tasks — Tap a task to start a 25-min Work countdown. When it hits 0:00 it auto-transitions to a 5-min Good Break (or a 15-min Sweet Long Break after every 4 work sessions). Tap the same task again to stop early. Each full work + break is one entry on the Activity Log and one band on the gauge.

Mana Multiplier / Muncher — Mana Multiplier rows (Drink water, 25 mins focus, …) apply their score to your Internal focus on tap. Mana Muncher rows apply to External. Two timer-mancher items (Youtube/Netflix, Unrelated news) tap to start counting up; tap again to stop. Total time recorded; external penalty applied at −3 per 5 minutes. Click and hold or use Add to create custom multipliers/munchers with any signed score.

Activity log — Below Mana Factors. Every timer (task pomodoros and muncher trackers) is recorded line by line with start–end times, duration, and any score penalty applied.

Statistics page

List view — Sessions grouped by date. Each session shows ext/int focus scores, tasks completed, completion %, and a mini vertical gauge of that session's time bands.

Weekly view — 7 columns (Mon–Sun) with shared 6 AM – 12 AM y-axis. Each day's color-coded segments visible at a glance. Use ← / → to navigate weeks.

Erase all data — Wipes both local and cloud data after confirmation.

Account & Cloud sync

Sign up with email + password. Your session stays signed in across page refreshes; your data — tasks, gauge segments, scores, settings, statistics — automatically syncs across devices via Supabase.
